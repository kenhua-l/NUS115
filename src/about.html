NUS115

The National University of Singapore (NUS) will be celebrating its 115th year of founding in 2020. A the island's first instituition of higher education instituition, NUS has nurtured generations of leaders and luminaries in Singapore and Asia that have contributed to the country and society.

With NUS115, we proudly celebrate the University's transformative impact over 115 years and its continual efforts at shaping the future for a better world. A series of signiture events and initiatives will take place throughout 2020 to mark this special milestone.
