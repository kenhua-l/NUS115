Message from NUS President

As we look back at the University's rich history of contribution to nation and society, we take heart that a tradition of excellence and a culture of innovation underscore all our endeavours and achievements.

In recent years, we have been strengthening our capabilities in education, research and entrepreneurship, as well as putting in place a best-in-class administration. One hundred and fifteen years on, we will continue to offer a top quality transformative education; undertake robust research that improves lives and creates better understanding; and innovate for a better Singapore, and a better world.

NUS remains committed to shaping the future. We welcome you to join us on this journey as we fulfil our aspiration to be among the most innovative Universities in the world.
